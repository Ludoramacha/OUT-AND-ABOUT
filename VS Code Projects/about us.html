Our Journey

Connecting students with affordable, exciting experiences in Gaborone.

Our Story

Out And About started with a simple idea,to make exploration effortless and budget-friendly for students. As students ourselves, we often found it hard to discover hidden gems, cozy caf√©s, and outdoor escapes without breaking the bank.

What started as a small Instagram page recommending student-friendly spots,grew into a full platform, connecting students with local businesses and cultural attractions. Today, we are dedicated to making affordable travel accessible, helping students experience the best of Gaborone without limits.

Our Mission

We believe that exploration should be accessible to everyone, no matter the budget. Our mission is to provide insider insights on local experiences, uncovering the best places to study, relax, and adventure.

Through authentic student stories, curated guides, and immersive video tours, we aim to help students experience Gaborone like never before.
